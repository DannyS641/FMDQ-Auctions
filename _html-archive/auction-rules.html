FMDQ Auctions Portal

Auction Rules

This document outlines the rules that govern auction participation and bidding activity on the portal.

1. Eligibility

Only registered users with the appropriate assigned role may participate in bidding activities.

2. Bidding increments

All bids must meet the configured starting bid and increment requirements for the relevant item.

3. Reserve conditions

Where a reserve price exists, the seller is not obliged to conclude a sale below that threshold.

4. Auction timing

Bids submitted before the opening time or after the closing time of an auction item are invalid.

5. Anonymous bidding

Bids may be displayed anonymously to participants, while administrator and audit records retain authoritative internal references.
