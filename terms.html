Access to the portal is restricted to approved users with valid platform accounts. Users are responsible for the security of their credentials and all actions performed under their accounts.

Participation in any auction is subject to compliance with the applicable auction rules, item-specific terms, and any instructions issued by FMDQ or an authorized administrator.

Item descriptions, documents, and images are provided for convenience. Users are expected to carry out their own due diligence before bidding.

Any bid placed through the portal may be treated as binding, subject to the applicable rules and reserve conditions attached to the auction.

FMDQ may suspend, amend, or withdraw access to the portal or any auction process where required for operational, legal, or risk-management reasons.
